Our Story

About bottlr

bottlr is a pickup coordination product built for collectors and hosts.

Our Mission

Make bottle pickup operations simpler, faster, and more transparent for everyone involved.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Clarity

Pickup state, claims, and completion status should be obvious at a glance.

Reliability

Flows should work quickly on mobile and stay stable under real use.

Fairness

Collectors and hosts both need predictable, transparent interactions.

Simplicity

Keep product and communication focused on what users actually need.

Product Principles

How We Build

Mobile-first interfaces

Core actions should be quick on phone screens and clear at every step.

Transparent status

Users should always know whether a job is posted, claimed, or completed.

Iterative improvements

We ship practical updates based on real workflow feedback.
