the story

why bottlr exists.

the problem

California's bottle deposit system generates $1.5 billion in redemption value annually — but 40% of bottles are never returned. Not because people don't care, but because the logistics were never built for the people doing the work. Collectors walk blind routes. Hosts pile bottles with no pickup scheduled. Redemption centers sit underutilized.

the solution

bottlr is the coordination layer that was missing. Hosts post pickup availability in under a minute. Collectors see live jobs on a map before leaving home. Every pickup is verified and logged. The app is access, not the product — we're building the operating layer cities use for SB 1383 compliance and enterprises use for ESG reporting.

the enterprise angle: why bottlr isn't a gig app

The real-time data layer story: ESG compliance, SB 1383, EPR. Governments and corporations need verified recovery data — bottlr delivers it at scale.

the founder story

Built at SF Hacks — a hackathon project that became the seed of a circular economy infrastructure company. Scaling from hackathon to infrastructure play.
