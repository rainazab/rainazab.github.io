The Problem

The System is Broken

California's $1.5 billion bottle redemption industry exploits workers, wastes resources, and fails communities.

$15

Poverty Wages

Collectors walk 5+ miles daily through trash for just $15 in 6 hours. No benefits. No dignity. No future.

$600M

Wasted Value

40% of bottles never get redeemed. Restaurants dump hundreds weekly while collectors search empty-handed.

ZERO

Zero Power

Collectors have no voice, no organization, no bargaining power. An invisible workforce exploited by a broken system.

The Solution

Power to the Collectors

A worker-first marketplace that connects collectors directly with high-value opportunities. Fair pricing. Transparent work. Community strength.

01

Real-Time Jobs

See every available bottle pickup on a live map. No more wandering. No more digging through trash. Claim high-value jobs instantly.

02

Fair Pay, Guaranteed

$179 average weekly earnings. Track every dollar in real-time. No hidden fees. No wage theft. Your work, your money.

03

Build Your Rep

Earn ratings, unlock recurring contracts, access premium routes. Top collectors earn 3x average. Merit matters.

04

Collective Power

150,000 collectors, one network. Track community impact. Build solidarity. Organize for better conditions.
