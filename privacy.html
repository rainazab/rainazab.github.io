privacy policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

what we collect

  • Account information you provide, such as name and email.
  • Activity information related to posted and claimed pickups.
  • Approximate or precise location data only when needed for map and matching features.

how we use data

  • Operate core product features and job matching.
  • Improve reliability, safety, and support.
  • Communicate product updates or service messages.

data sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We may share data with service providers that help run the platform.
  • We may disclose data when legally required.

your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • You can disable location permissions in your device settings.

contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@bottlr.tech.

Privacy Policy

Essential privacy details for collectors and hosts using bottlr.

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

What We Collect

  • Account info (name, email, phone)
  • Pickup data (job posts, claims, completion status)
  • Location data only for map and matching features

How We Use It

  • Match collectors and hosts
  • Show job and activity status
  • Improve reliability and support

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell personal data
  • We do not share user data for ad targeting

Your Controls

  • Update profile details in account settings
  • Request account/data deletion
  • Disable location access in device settings

Contact

For privacy requests, use the project contact channel listed in the repository.
