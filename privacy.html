Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. Here's how we protect it.

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

At bottlr, we believe your data is yours. We collect only what's necessary to operate the platform, never sell your information, and give you full control over your data.

Information We Collect

For Collectors:

  • Name, phone number, and email (for account creation)
  • Location data (only when using the map feature)
  • Pickup history and earnings (visible to you at all times)
  • Ratings and reviews from donors
  • Payment information (encrypted and processed by our payment partner)

For Donors:

  • Name, email, and phone number
  • Pickup address (visible only to matched collectors)
  • Donation history and impact metrics

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the platform: Matching collectors with donors, processing payments
  • For safety: Verifying users, investigating reported issues
  • To improve service: Analyzing aggregate usage patterns (never individual data)
  • For support: Responding to your inquiries and resolving issues

What We DON'T Do

  • ❌ We never sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ We never share your information with advertisers
  • ❌ We never track you across other websites or apps
  • ❌ We never use your data for purposes you haven't consented to

Data Sharing

We only share your data in these limited circumstances:

  • With matched users: Collectors see donor pickup addresses; donors see collector names and ratings
  • With payment processors: To process payments securely
  • For legal compliance: If required by law or to protect user safety

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: Download all your data anytime through the app
  • Correction: Update your information in your profile settings
  • Deletion: Request account deletion (processed within 30 days)
  • Opt-out: Control what communications you receive
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format

Location Data

Location tracking is essential for our service, but we respect your privacy:

  • Location is collected only when you're using the map feature
  • You can disable location access in your phone settings anytime
  • We don't track your location in the background
  • Historical location data is automatically deleted after 90 days

Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • Industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (SOC 2 certified)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Minimal data retention (we keep only what's necessary)

Children's Privacy

bottlr is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If we make significant changes, we'll notify you via email and in-app notification 30 days before they take effect.
