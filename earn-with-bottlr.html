how the money works

you keep what you collect.

California's CRV (California Redemption Value) is a bottle deposit paid when you buy a beverage — and redeemable when you return the bottle. bottlr routes unclaimed bottles to collectors who do the pickup and redemption. 100% of that CRV is yours.

for common bottles and cans

CRV varies by container type and material. Aluminum cans, glass bottles, and plastic containers all have their own redemption rates. bottlr shows you the bottle count so you can plan the value of a pickup before you claim it.

no deductions, no surprises

What you take to the redemption center is what you keep. bottlr charges businesses a monthly fee to post pickups — collectors pay nothing and lose nothing off the top.
