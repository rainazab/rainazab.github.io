the network

one local flow.
hosts. collectors.
value returned.

The bottlr network is a closed loop: businesses post, collectors claim, redemption centers process, and value flows back into the community. Live, local, and transparent.

the flow

four actors. one clean loop.

Every pickup on bottlr follows the same path — from host posting to collector earnings, with verification at every step.

H

hosts post

Businesses, households, and event venues post pickup availability: location, bottle count, and time window. Visible instantly on the live map.

C

collectors claim

Collectors nearby see the posting, evaluate the route and count, and claim with one tap. The host gets a notification. The job is locked.

P

pickup + verify

The collector completes the pickup and submits photo verification through the app. Both sides get a timestamped completion record.

$

payout + impact

The collector redeems bottles and keeps 100% of CRV. The host gets an impact summary. The network records the outcome.

network principles

designed for trust at scale.

The bottlr network works because every actor has clear incentives and every action is verified. There's no ambiguity about who picked up what, or when.

photo verification on every job

Collectors submit a photo at pickup. This creates an immutable record for the host and an accountability layer for the network.

real-time job locking

Once claimed, a job is locked to that collector. No double-claiming, no race conditions, no wasted trips.

transparent completion history

Both collectors and hosts see their full history: completed pickups, quantities, timing, and outcomes. No black boxes.

network economics

how the money moves.

bottlr charges businesses a flat monthly fee to post pickups. Collectors pay nothing — and keep everything they redeem. That's the model.

$0
cost to collectors

Free to download, free to use, free forever. We never monetize the people doing the work.

100%
CRV to collector

Every bottle deposit goes to the collector who picks it up. Zero cut from bottlr.

flat
business pricing

Businesses pay a predictable monthly fee. No per-pickup fees, no hidden costs.

local

CRV money recirculates in the neighborhood — from host to collector to local spending.
