How It Works

bottlr connects bottle collectors with opportunities through a simple, transparent platform. Fair work. Fair pay. No exploitation.

Three Simple Steps

01

Download & Sign Up

Get the bottlr app on iOS or Android. Create your profile in minutes. No fees to join. No barriers to entry. Your work starts when you're ready.

  • Free to download and use
  • Quick verification process
  • Works on any smartphone
  • Available in multiple languages
02

Find Jobs on the Map

See real-time bottle pickup opportunities near you. Each listing shows location, bottle count, and payout. Claim jobs instantly‚Äîfirst come, first served.

  • Live map with all available jobs
  • Filter by distance and payout
  • One-tap job claiming
  • Route optimization included
03

Collect & Get Paid

Pick up bottles, verify collection with a photo, and head to your nearest redemption center. Earnings tracked in real-time. Get paid weekly via direct deposit.

  • Photo verification for transparency
  • Weekly direct deposits
  • No hidden fees or deductions
  • Build reputation for better jobs
